### Accordion.Trigger Props

Accordion.Trigger renders a `button` and composes `Box`, this means you can
style it by passing the pseudo style props `_expanded`, `_disabled`, `_hover`,
etc.

### Accordion.Content Props

Accordion.Content renders a `div` and composes `Collapse` to provide the height
animation.
