## 👋🏽 A bit of context

[TM Code](https://code.toquemedia.net) is an agent first IDE where a developer
describes what they want built, and the agent writes the code, shows the diff,
opens a live preview, and deploys the project.

Célio Garcia is the CEO and founder of TM Code. Before building TM code, he was
familiar with mobile UI frameworks like Jetpack Compose and SwiftUI. He
understood how those tools worked and their approach, so finding the right UI
library for his IDE was one of the first decisions he had to make.

That search led him to Chakra UI.

<QuoteCard
  quote="It reminds me a lot of Jetpack Compose and SwiftUI, that's why I loved Chakra UI on day one."

/>

## 💙 Fav thing about Chakra UI

With Chakra, he didn't need to spend time learning a new way of building
interfaces. Instead, he could focus on the product itself. When we asked what
Chakra let him do faster or better than he expected, Célio said: **"Definitely
user interfaces."**

Chakra v3's theming was the early hurdle. The v3 release changed how themes are
defined, but once he understood the new approach, Chakra carried the rest of the
interface work through to launch at
[code.toquemedia.net](https://code.toquemedia.net). Nothing else slowed him
down.
